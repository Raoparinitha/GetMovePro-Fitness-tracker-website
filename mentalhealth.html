Anxiety

Anxiety is a natural human response to stress or perceived threats. It's a normal part of life and can serve as a protective mechanism, alerting us to potential dangers and helping us respond appropriately. However, when anxiety becomes chronic, overwhelming, or disproportionate to the situation, it can develop into an anxiety disorder, negatively impacting daily life.
